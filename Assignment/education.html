Fatulla Pilot High School

I have passed JSC and SSC from this School

Fatulla Pilot High School was established in 1895. It is well known school in Narayangonj. It provides high school level education, as a double shifted boys school providing education from class six to Secondary School Certificate examination (SSC) under three groups: Science, Arts and Commerce. Every year a large number of students are getting admitted through entrance exam in this school.

Govt. Tolaram College, the greatest educational institution of the district Narayangonj, was established at the centre of the town in 1955. It was nationalized in 1979 and since then it has been working to develop the human resource of this area to be a skilled one.
